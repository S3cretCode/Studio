Unveiling the scientific synergy that powers play

Discover the intricate dance between chemistry and physics that brings a simple RC toy car to life. From the hidden chemical reactions within its batteries to the magnetic forces that spin its motor, we explore the real-world science behind everyday technology.

Project Context & Learning Objectives

Battery Chemistry (Chem 30S)

Understand how AA alkaline battery chemistry (Zn / MnO₂) governs available charge and energy through stoichiometry.

Physics of Motion (Physics 30S)

Explore how chemical energy transforms into electric current, creating magnetic fields that drive a DC motor.

Interdisciplinary Connection

Stoichiometry → Charge → Current → Magnetic Field → Motion. See how chemistry limits physics.
