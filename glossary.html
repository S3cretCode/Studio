Glossary

Anode

The electrode where oxidation (electron loss) occurs. In alkaline batteries, this is Zinc.

Cathode

The electrode where reduction (electron gain) occurs. In alkaline batteries, this is Manganese Dioxide.

Current (Amps)

The rate of flow of electric charge. 1 Ampere = 1 Coulomb per second.

Stoichiometry

The math of chemistry using balanced equations to calculate reactant/product quantities.

Faraday's Constant

96,485 C/mol. The amount of charge in one mole of electrons.

Back EMF

A voltage generated by a spinning motor that opposes the battery voltage, limiting speed.
